========================================================================
                      GO STEALTH — USER MANUAL & SETUP GUIDE
========================================================================
Welcome to Go Stealth, the invisible AI Interview Copilot designed by 
RiddleTech to assist you during live technical interviews.

This manual will guide you through setting up and using all features.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. CONFIGURATION & FIRST-TIME SETUP
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Before using Go Stealth, you must configure your AI keys. 

1. Launch Go Stealth.
2. Click the SETTINGS GEAR icon in the top toolbar.
3. Paste your API keys for the services you wish to use:
   * Google Gemini (Free key at ai.google.dev)
   * Groq (Free key at console.groq.com)
   * Deepgram (Free real-time voice key at console.deepgram.com)
4. Click "Test Connection" to verify that your keys work.
5. Click "Save All Settings".

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. KEY FEATURES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* VISION AI (Screen Capture): 
  Select your interview window (Zoom, Teams, Chrome) from the drop-down 
  selector. Click "Capture Screen" to instantly snapshot and extract the 
  question text using Vision AI.

* SPEECH-TO-TEXT (Mic Listening):
  Click "Listen" to transcribe the interviewer's voice in real-time. 
  The app listens, transcribes, and keeps the text ready for analysis.

* GENERATE ANSWERS:
  Click the "Analyze" button to trigger the AI model to generate 
  detailed solutions based on the captured screenshot or audio.

* STEALTH MODE (Invisibility):
  Turn off "Be Visible" in the footer, or press the hotkey, to make the 
  entire application window completely invisible to screensharing, 
  screenshots, or video recording software!

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Use these global keyboard hotkeys for maximum discretion:
* Ctrl + Shift + C  — Capture screen & analyze
* Ctrl + Shift + L  — Toggle microphone listening
* Ctrl + Shift + X  — Clear answer screen
* Ctrl + Shift + S  — Toggle Stealth Mode (Invisibility)

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. SUBSCRIPTION & REGISTRATION
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Your app starts with a free Trial. To upgrade to Pro:
1. Click the Shield Icon in the top toolbar.
2. Choose a plan and complete payment via PayPal.
3. If you have an admin-generated License Key, enter it in the 
   "Redeem License" box and click "Activate Key".
4. If you reinstall the app, simply check subscription or re-enter 
   your key; it will automatically restore.
